About

A scientist who refuses to look away.

Dr. Gad Saad is a scholar at the Declaration of Independence Center for the Study of American Freedom at the University of Mississippi, and Professor of Marketing at Concordia University in Montreal, where he held the Research Chair in Evolutionary Behavioral Sciences and Darwinian Consumption for a decade.

He has pioneered the application of evolutionary psychology to marketing and consumer behavior, with 75+ scientific papers spanning psychology, medicine, economics, and advertising. He has held visiting professorships at Cornell, Dartmouth, and UC Irvine.

Beyond academia, Gad is one of the most prominent public intellectuals challenging the ideological conformity and "idea pathogens" eroding reason and science in modern discourse.
